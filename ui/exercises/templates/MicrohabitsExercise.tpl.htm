Examples: Exercise more, meditate, learn a language, get organized, write a novel, read literature, practice guitar.

What benefits will you get if you do this consistently?

Example: Exercising consistently will give me more energy, less body fat, a better mood and better health. I could be the star player on my soccer team. I'll look better on the beach. I'll set a good example for my little brother.

What's the best time for you to do it?

Picking a specific time helps wire the habit into your brain.

Now create a microhabit. It should be easy and take 1 minute or less to complete. It could be the first thing you would do when starting this activity.

Examples: Change into my running clothes, meditate for 1 minute, read one paragraph of my book, tune my guitar.

Remember!

Your only obligation is to complete the 1-minute microhabit. If your microhabit is to put on your running clothes, you don't actually have to go running! Anything else you do is extra credit. As you continue to complete your microhabit, you'll find it will grow larger and larger over time.
