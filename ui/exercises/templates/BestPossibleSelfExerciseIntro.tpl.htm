Best Possible Self

"Best Possible Self" is a powerful psychological exercise that has been proven to increase motivation, happiness and optimism. It's an excellent first exercise because it unleashes a flood of motivation that will get you started on your journey of positive mental transformation.


By identifying your ideal self at your happiest and most successful, you give your brain a compelling vision of the future to work towards, which will help you achieve greater results in all aspects of your life.
