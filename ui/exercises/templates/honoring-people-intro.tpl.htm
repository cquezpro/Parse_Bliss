Honoring people in your life

The strength of your relationships is one of the strongest predictors of happiness. Our social bonds help us weather difficult times, and make the good times even better. However, it's all too easy to take people for granted and forget how lucky we are to have them. In this next exercise, you'll pick a specific person you're grateful for and write a short paragraph on the specific things about them you appreciate most. This trains your brains to focus on the positive aspects of your relationships, helps you be a better friend, and ultimately strengthens the relationship.
